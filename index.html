SceneSetter is a card-based visual story organizer for writers. Get your characters, locations, themes, and scenes in order so you can focus on telling your best story.
Free to use · No account required
Your content remains privately yours. All data is stored locally in your browser, per project. (We use Google Analytics and other tools to understand feature usage and improve the app, but no personal data or project content is shared.)
